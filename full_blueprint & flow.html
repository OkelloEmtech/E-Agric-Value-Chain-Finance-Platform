CONTROLLED DISBURSEMENT HUB
Sub-Wallet Allocation (Anchor approval required for Suppliers & Aggregators)

Sub-Wallet Structure per Actor

⚓
Anchor Sub-Wallet Receives: Buyer payments for produce + Farmer loan repayments.
Sends: Approval signal to Master Wallet to release funds to Suppliers & Aggregators. Repays Master Wallet (principal + interest only) — Master Wallet settles to Bank via waterfall.
Retains: Anchor commission — stays on Anchor Sub-Wallet after waterfall settlement.
🌾
Farmer Sub-Wallets Receives: Input financing (disbursed directly to Supplier via Master Wallet). Seasonal crop advances.
Sends: Repayment to Anchor Sub-Wallet at harvest (deducted from produce payment).
🏭
Supplier Sub-Wallets Receives: Full input financing from Master Wallet (100% of input cost, paid directly upon Anchor approval). Bank loan covers the full amount.
Note: Master Wallet retains 20% of input cost at disbursement — not paid out immediately.
Receives at settlement: 20% contribution paid back from Master Wallet once produce is delivered & verified.
Delivers inputs to Farmers.
🚛
Aggregator Sub-Wallets Receives: Transport & aggregation fees from Master Wallet upon Anchor approval.
Sends: Aggregated produce to VC Anchor. Delivery confirmation triggers fee release.
Sub-Wallets activate within VC Anchor ecosystem
③ VC Anchor — Ecosystem Orchestrator
⚓ VC Anchor / Processor / Buyer
  • Central hub of the entire value chain ecosystem
  • Holds its own Anchor Sub-Wallet (within Master Wallet)
  • RECEIVES MONEY: Buyer payments · Farmer repayments (into Anchor Sub-Wallet)
  • RETAINS: Commission stays on Anchor Sub-Wallet after waterfall
  • APPROVES: Master Wallet releases 80% to Suppliers upfront · retains 20% until settlement · pays 20% back to Suppliers at settlement
  • APPROVES: Supplier payment releases from Master Wallet
  • APPROVES: Aggregator fee releases from Master Wallet
  • Off-take agreements with Farmers & Retailers
  • Monitors: Purchase volume · Recovery ratio · Contract adherence
  • Commission earned stays on Anchor Sub-Wallet — not passed to Bank
  • Submits ecosystem creation, credit, float requests for bank approval

⑦ Settlement Waterfall (Configurable Priority Order)

🌊 Settlement Waterfall Engine — Deduction Hierarchy

1 · Insurance →
2 · Supplier Recovery →
3 · Bank Principal →
4 · Bank Interest →
5 · Farmer Residual
⚓ Anchor Commission — NOT part of waterfall. Retained directly on Anchor Sub-Wallet after all waterfall deductions are complete.
